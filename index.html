Cat

Cats can rotate their ears 180 degrees, which helps them detect sounds with great accuracy.

  • Scientific Name: Felis catus
  • Average Length: 46 cm (18 inches) excluding the tail
  • Average Lifespan: 12-15 years
  • Habitat: Domestic environments and wild areas

Known for their agility and independence, cats are one of the most popular pets worldwide. They have sharp senses, particularly their keen hearing and vision, which make them exceptional hunters. Domestic cats have been companions to humans for thousands of years.
